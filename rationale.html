Rationale

Has the US failed nurses in reslience training during COVID & have they failed each other with worklife culture?

Alyssa Thompson

Spring HON 202

Questions I am examining and an exploring in my claims against resilience

If the programs currently in place will effectively address the psychological burden on HCW during the pandemic.

What HCW state their needs are and if the consensus is positive

Underlying occupational social structure influencing these factors

The goal will be to bring awareness to the mental health of HCW and the lack of a strong quantitative proven framework to addressing psychological well-being. My hypothesis, as well as first hand experience, is there is a large gap in care and support for HCW in the United States and is further exacerbated by the occupational social culture of the hospital system. The cited lived experience of HCWs cited in the large body of psychologically impact of COVID will be the foundation for data collection. The data I hope to find is supportive evidence from HCWs that create the concept to prove my hypothesis.
